                      DATABASE STRUCTURE
===============================================================================

data/video_games.csv
├── Game Info          : Game_ID, Game_Name
├── Category          : Genre, Platform, Publisher
├── Release           : Release_Year
├── Commercial        : Price_USD, Sales_Million, Revenue_Million
├── Players           : Player_Count
├── Ratings           : Rating, Metacritic_Score
├── Development       : Development_Cost_Million, Playtime_Hours
├── Financial         : Copies_Sold_Million, ROI_Percent
└── Engagement        : Engagement_Score

Total: 500 games × 17 columns
